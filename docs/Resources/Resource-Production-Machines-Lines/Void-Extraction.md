# Void Extraction

<small>**Guide by:** Jkj3000</small>

Void extraction is the best way to get several materials otherwise inaccessible. You can also further refine the liquids gotten to get rarer resources as well as more of the common resources.



???+ abstract "Without any further refining you can get:"

    - Raw Coal
    - Raw Gold
    - Raw Sodalite
    - Raw Pentlandite/Platlite
    - Raw Realgar
    - Raw Silver

???+ abstract "With further refining you can get"
   
    - Chromite/Chromium
    - Rare Earth:

        - Cadmium
        - Neodymium
        - Samarium
        - Cerium
        - Yttrium
        - Lanthanum
    - Vanadium Magnetite
    - Cobaltite/Cobalt

    - Bauxite/Aluminum
    - Pitchblende
    - Molybdenite
    - Ilmenite
    - Bastnasite
    - Tungstate/Tungsten
    - Sheldonite/Platline

    - Tantalite/Tantalum
    - Pollucite
    - Cassiterite

    - Barite
    - Chalcopyrite/Platline
    - Bornite/Platline

    - Beryllium
    - Zavaritskite

    - Lepidolite/Potassium/Lithium
    - Pyrochlore/Niobium
    - Pyrolusite/Manganese
    - Magnesite/Magnesium
